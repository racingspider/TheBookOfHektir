The Lair of Goleckeum

The use of magic causes a buildup of ruin, a trace magical scent that beings from other realities track down to devour the spirit it is attached to. Wizard Apprentices are taught ways to purge their spirits of ruin.

After a few weeks of uneventful travel, you have arrived at the entrance to what is believed to be Goleckeum's ancient lair. Goleckeum, the ancient Ruin Drake slew a great many sorcerers and spell casters.

Deep in the bowels of this forgotten ruin lies the fabled "Tapestry Room". Rumors tell of engravings on the walls that solves a magical riddle.

Select 6 tokens (of any combination) in preparation. Create 3 new characters, or use existing ones.

  • Journal - (knowledge token)

  • Scroll - (magic token)

  • Map portions - (navigation token)

  • Torch - (cunning token)

  • Toolkit - (action token)
