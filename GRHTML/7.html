Lashing tentacles, creeping madness, some spit, some spray, but ALL BITE!

Tunnel Slugs and Lash Slugs are common in wet, dismal abandoned places. Tunnel Slugs are generally larger than their Lash Slug cousins, but usually less lethal. Lash Slugs tend to have a multitude of stinging, toothed tentacles.

"The writheing thing spun in wild circles as though horrifically injured as it's great mass extruded from a small hole in the wall. We held our collective breath as it plopped to the floor and remained stationary for a moment. It seemed to have decided we were a worthy meal as it crashed toward us with abandon."

In a Combat encounter, the crew must gain "advantage" as many times as the creature's health.

To gain advantage, play cards to match the icons next to the Flag (advantage). Only one advantage can be gained at a time.

Advantages Required: 2 [3 for difficult]

Advantage: Fight + Navigation + Physical Die

Dice: 2 Gruesome, 2 Mundane

  • Play 2 max
