GHOULS!

The air went still, tentative shuffling of sneaking steps, raspy, guttural snufflings eerily echoed in a whisper from down the hall. With both a fear of moving closer, while still being drawn to our living breath, the flash of dog-like faces revealed themselves momentarily from the darkness, eyes glinting in the torchlight.

A stench crept forward, dazing us briefly. With no sounds but the scrabbling of their nails on the stone, they were upon us. Gnashing teeth in snouted mouths, the canine horrors thrashed among us wildly.
